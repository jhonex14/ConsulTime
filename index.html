How ConsulTime Works

Get started in minutes with our streamlined booking workflow designed for both students and faculty.

1
Create Account

Register as a Student or Faculty member using your university email credentials. Faculty requests are safely vetted by administrators.

2
Set or Pick Slots

Faculty post their free days and times. Students search by course, department, or professor name to pick a matching free slot instantly.

3
Sync & Meet

Get approved, receive status updates, and conduct the consultation session. Keep your academic calendar organized effortlessly.
